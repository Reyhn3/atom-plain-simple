[Plain And Simple Text]
	This package does not "format" the text - it simply _colors_ it!
	Coloring adds **visual** formatting to make it simpler to read.

	[Some Highlights]
		Dates (2017-12-24) and numbers (+1, -3.14, 0xFF etc.) are
		identified. URLs http://atom.io/ and IP-addresses 192.168.0.1
		are identified, as well as e-mail addresses package@atom.io
		and other tokens.

			*	Colored
			*	Highlights

			*	Reading
			*	Easier!
